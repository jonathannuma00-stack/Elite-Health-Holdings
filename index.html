Our Approach

We focus on acquiring and partnering with exceptional healthcare companies while preserving their culture, empowering their teams, and aligning with like-minded business owners. We help sellers with their succession plans and ensure a smooth transition, building a collaborative network within a 2-hour geographic radius to foster growth, operational excellence, and long-term success.

Acquisition Criteria

Flexible structures. Founder-friendly transitions. Speed with diligence.

  • ✔️ EBITDA: $0.5M–$5M+ (Lower EBITDA considered on a case-by-case basis)
  • ✔️ Mix: Medicare-certified home health and hospice
  • ✔️ Geography: Sunbelt & growth markets (multi-state)
  • ✔️ Quality: Clean surveys, compliant billing, strong PDGM/PEPPER profile
  • ✔️ Culture: Patient-first, clinician-supported
  • ✔️ Deal types: Majority buyouts, recapitalizations, carve-outs
  • ✔️ Timing: 30–60 day closes for qualified sellers
  • ✔️ Post-close: Sellers can roll equity & advise
