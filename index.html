Our Roll-Up Strategy

We target established healthcare businesses and integrate them into a centralized holding structure, creating operational synergies and enhancing market presence in each geographic hub. Our first acquisition anchors the hub, with additional acquisitions within a two-hour radius to strengthen local market dominance.

Acquisition Criteria

  • Industry: Healthcare (Home Health, Hospice, Specialized Clinics, Medical Practices)
  • EBITDA: $500k to $5M (Lower EBITDA considered case-by-case)
  • Strong operational history and reputation for quality care
  • Located in high-growth or strategically valuable markets
